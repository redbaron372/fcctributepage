Abraham Lincoln

16th President of the United States


  • 1809 - Abraham Lincoln is born in Kentucky on Feb 12th.
  • 1817 - Abraham Lincoln's family settled in Indiana.
  • 1831 - Linoln worked for himself. His work included: boat building, sailing, carpentry, hog sticking, sawmilling, and logging. He also began studying the law.
  • 1842 - Abraham Lincoln married Miss Mary Todd of Kentucky. They had four sons together.
  • 1856 - Organized the Republican Party, Nominated Vice-President, but was not chosen for its first convention.
  • 1861 - Inaugurated President. Abraham Linconln became the 16th President of the United States of America on March 4th. Civil war began on April 12th.
  • 1863 - Emancipation Proclimation. On January 1st, emancipation was proclaimed. Lincoln delivered a famous speech known as The Gettysburg Address. This occurred on November 19th on the battlefield near Gettysburg, Pennsylvania.
  • 1864 - Abraham Lincoln was reelected on November 8th as the Republican President. It was a unanimous nomination.
  • 1865 - Lincoln was inaugurated on March 4th for his second presidential term. He was then assassinated on April 14th in Ford's Theater by Wilkes Booth. Booth was slain on April 26th.
  • 1922 - The Lincoln Memorial was dedicated to Lincoln on May 30th. The building is a constant reminder of Lincoln's significance and valuable contributions to the history of the country. It is located in Washington D.C.
